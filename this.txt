Meditation offers a wide range of benefits for both the mind and body.  
Regular practice reduces stress by calming the nervous system and lowering cortisol levels.  
It enhances focus and concentration by training the mind to stay present.  
Meditation promotes self-awareness and emotional regulation.  
Physically, it lowers blood pressure, improves sleep, and strengthens immunity.  
Over time, it fosters inner peace and helps manage daily challenges with clarity and calm
